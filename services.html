Our Services

From telecommunications infrastructure to solar energy and civil works — SSS delivers comprehensive, professional-grade solutions across all 36 districts of Punjab.

Category 01

Telecommunications & Network Infrastructure

SSS provides comprehensive telecommunication services for Pakistan's growing connectivity needs — from fiber optic cable networks to telecom tower installations across Punjab.

We have successfully completed projects for PTCL, Naya Tell Telecommunication, and Telecom Foundation, with proven expertise in both aerial and underground installations.

Fiber Cable Hanging

Aerial fiber optic cable installation and suspension along utility poles and structures.

Cable Installation

Complete fiber cable installation services from planning to commissioning.

Cable Pulling & Connection

Underground duct cable pulling, termination, and live fiber connectivity services.

Telecom Tower Erection

Greenfield and rooftop telecom tower construction and equipment installation.

Tower Maintenance

Regular inspection, maintenance, and repair of existing telecom tower infrastructure.

Safety Training

Telecom site safety training, IEC material production, and video documentation.

Civil Works & Infrastructure

Underground Cable Duct

Water Supply Lines

Pipeline installation and water supply infrastructure for communities and institutions.

Electrical Fixtures

Industrial and commercial electrical installation and fixture services.

Construction Works

General construction, renovation, and building infrastructure projects.

Site Survey & Planning

Topographic surveys, site feasibility studies, and project planning.

Nationwide Coverage

Active presence across all 36 districts of Punjab through branch & partner offices.

Commercial Solar

Agricultural Solar

System Design & Audit
